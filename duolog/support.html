Duolog (雙向會議翻譯) — Support

Duolog 支援中心

FAQ | 常見問題

Q: What does Duolog do? | Duolog 是做什麼的？

Duolog is real-time, two-way meeting translation for your Mac. You speak Chinese and the other person reads English; they speak English and you instantly see Chinese. Supported pairs: Chinese ↔ English, Chinese ↔ Japanese, English ↔ Japanese.

Duolog 是 Mac 上的即時雙向會議翻譯。你說中文、對方看英文；對方說英文、你即時看中文。支援中↔英、中↔日、英↔日。

Q: Do I need an account or sign-in? | 需要註冊或登入嗎？

No. Just open Duolog and press Start. Every day includes 10 free minutes of translation. Signing into Google Drive is optional (only for syncing meeting notes to your own Drive).

不用。打開 Duolog 按開始即可。每天免費 10 分鐘。登入 Google Drive 為選填（僅用於把會議筆記同步到你自己的 Drive）。

Q: Does it need internet? | 需要網路嗎？

Speech recognition runs on-device (your audio stays on your Mac). Translation of the recognized text uses an online service (a free path, or your own Gemini / DeepL key), so an internet connection is needed for translation.

語音辨識在本機執行（音訊留在你的 Mac）。辨識後的文字翻譯使用線上服務（免費路徑或你自備的 Gemini / DeepL 金鑰），翻譯需要連網。

Q: Why does the App Store version's accuracy differ? | 為何 App Store 版精度不同？

The App Store edition uses macOS’ built-in on-device speech recognition for full sandbox compatibility. Accuracy varies by language and accent; proper nouns and punctuation may be weaker. Recommended: an Apple Silicon Mac, macOS 13 or later, 8 GB RAM or more, for the best real-time experience.

App Store 版採用 macOS 內建的本機語音辨識以完整相容沙盒。辨識精度因語言與口音而異，專有名詞與標點可能較弱。建議配置：Apple Silicon Mac、macOS 13 以上、記憶體 8 GB 以上。

Q: What permissions does Duolog request, and why? | Duolog 需要哪些權限？

Microphone — captures your own voice for on-device speech recognition. Screen & System Audio Recording — used only to capture the other party’s meeting audio for translation; Duolog does not capture, store, or transmit your screen contents.

麥克風—擷取你自己的聲音做本機語音辨識。螢幕與系統錄製—僅用於擷取對方的會議語音做翻譯；Duolog 不截取、不儲存、不傳送你的畫面內容。

Q: How do subscriptions and “Restore Purchases” work? | 訂閱與「回復購買」怎麼用？

The free tier gives 10 minutes per day. Unlimited is available via an auto-renewable monthly or yearly subscription, managed by the App Store. If you reinstall or use another Mac, open Settings and tap Restore Purchases. Manage or cancel anytime in System Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions.

免費版每天 10 分鐘。無限版透過 App Store 的自動續訂月費或年費訂閱。重裝或換 Mac 時，到設定按「回復購買」。可隨時在系統設定 → 你的 Apple 帳號 → 訂閱 管理或取消。

Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature? | 如何回報問題或建議功能？

Email us with a description, steps to reproduce (for bugs), and your macOS version. We read every message.

請來信附上問題描述、重現步驟（如為錯誤）及你的 macOS 版本。我們會閱讀每一封來信。
