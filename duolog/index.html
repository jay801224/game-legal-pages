Duolog (雙向會議翻譯) — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Duolog (two-way meeting translation) is privacy-first. The developer does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers — we have none. Speech recognition runs locally on your computer, and your API keys stay on your machine.

Duolog（雙向會議翻譯）以「本機優先」設計。開發者不收集、不儲存、不傳送你的個人資料到我們的伺服器（我們沒有伺服器）。語音辨識在你的電腦本機執行，你的 API 金鑰只存在你電腦本機。

1. How Data Is Handled | 我們如何處理資料

Data Type Details Purpose
Microphone / Audio
麥克風 / 音訊		 Captured for real-time translation. Speech recognition (Whisper) runs locally on your computer. Recognized text is sent to the third-party translation service you configure (e.g. Google / Gemini / DeepL). Audio and content are never sent to the developer.
為即時翻譯而擷取；Whisper 在本機執行；辨識文字送往你自設的第三方翻譯服務；音訊與內容不會傳給開發者。		 Real-time translation only
API Keys (BYO key)
API 金鑰（自帶）		 Stored only locally on your machine (%LOCALAPPDATA%\Duolog); used to call your own translation account; never uploaded to the developer.
只存在你電腦本機，用來呼叫你自己的翻譯服務帳號，永不上傳給開發者。		 Call your own translation service
Transcripts / Notes
逐字稿 / 筆記		 Generated locally. If you opt into cloud notes, uploaded to your own Google Drive account (with your authorization, scope drive.file only). Stored solely in your own cloud — the developer has no access.
產生於本機；若你選擇雲端筆記，則上傳到你自己的 Google Drive 帳號（需你授權，僅 drive.file 範圍），僅存於你自己的雲端，與開發者無關。		 Optional cloud backup of your notes
Error Reporting (Sentry)
錯誤回報		 Uncaught exceptions are reported for stability. No PII, no audio, no translation content (send_default_pii=False) — only technical crash/exception data.
未捕捉的程式例外會回報以提升穩定度；不含個人識別資訊、不含音訊、不含翻譯內容，僅技術性崩潰資訊。		 Stability & crash diagnostics
Payments
付款		 Subscriptions are processed by the Microsoft Store; payment information is managed by Microsoft. The developer never sees your payment data.
訂閱經由 Microsoft Store 處理，付款資訊由 Microsoft 管理；開發者不接觸你的付款資料。		 Subscription billing
Accounts
帳號		 The app requires no sign-up and collects no name / email / location.
app 本身不需註冊、不建立帳號，不收集姓名 / Email / 位置。		 —

2. Third-Party Services | 第三方服務

The translation service you choose, Google Drive, and the Microsoft Store each have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Drive / Google Sign-In — Google Privacy Policy
  • Your chosen translation service (Google / Gemini / DeepL, per your API key) — see that provider's policy
  • Microsoft Store — Microsoft Privacy Statement

3. Children's Privacy | 兒童隱私

Duolog is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

本 app 非針對 13 歲以下兒童設計，我們不會蓄意收集兒童的個人資訊。

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page.

本政策若有更新，會更新本頁頂端的日期。
