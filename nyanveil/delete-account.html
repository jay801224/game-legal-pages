NyanVeil (喵紗) — Delete Account

Last updated: April 10, 2026

How to Delete Your Account | 如何刪除帳號

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the NyanVeil app.

您可以直接在 NyanVeil App 內刪除帳號及所有相關資料。

Steps | 步驟

  1. Open NyanVeil and go to Settings (gear icon).
    打開 NyanVeil，進入「設定」（齒輪圖示）。
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
    向下滑動，點擊「刪除帳號」。
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
    確認刪除操作。
Warning | 警告

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. The following data will be permanently removed:

帳號刪除是永久且不可復原的。以下資料將被永久刪除：

  • Game progress and cat collection | 遊戲進度及貓咪收藏
  • Purchase history | 購買記錄
  • Cloud save data | 雲端備份資料
  • Account profile information | 帳號個人資訊
