NyanVeil 支援中心

FAQ | 常見問題

Q: How do I save my progress? | 如何儲存遊戲進度？

Your progress is saved automatically on your device. To enable cloud backup, go to Settings > Cloud Backup and sign in with your Apple or Google account.

您的進度會自動儲存在裝置上。如需啟用雲端備份，請前往「設定 > 雲端備份」並使用 Apple 或 Google 帳號登入。

Q: How do I restore purchases? | 如何恢復購買？

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

請前往「設定 > 恢復購買」。請確認您使用的是與原始購買相同的 Apple ID 或 Google 帳號。

Q: How do I delete my account? | 如何刪除帳號？

Go to Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data. For more details, see our Account Deletion page.

請前往「設定 > 刪除帳號」。此操作將永久移除您的所有資料。詳情請參閱帳號刪除頁面。

Q: The game is crashing. What should I do? | 遊戲當機怎麼辦？

Please try the following steps:

  1. Update NyanVeil to the latest version from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Restart your device.
  3. Clear the app cache (Settings > Storage > Clear Cache).
  4. If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and OS version.

請嘗試以下步驟：1. 從 App Store 或 Google Play 更新至最新版本。2. 重新啟動裝置。3. 清除應用程式快取（設定 > 儲存空間 > 清除快取）。4. 若問題持續，請聯絡我們並提供裝置型號與作業系統版本。

Q: How do I report a bug? | 如何回報錯誤？

You can report bugs through email or our Discord community. Please include a description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and your device information.

您可以透過電子郵件或 Discord 社群回報錯誤。請包含問題描述、重現步驟及裝置資訊。
