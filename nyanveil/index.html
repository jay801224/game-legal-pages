NyanVeil (喵紗) — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

NyanVeil ("the App") is a cat collection and raising game. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

NyanVeil (「本應用程式」) 是一款貓咪收集與養成遊戲。本隱私權政策說明我們如何收集、使用及保護您的資訊。

1. Information We Collect | 我們收集的資訊

Data Type Details Purpose
Account Information
帳號資訊		 Display name, email (from Apple/Google/Game Center login) Authentication, cloud save identification
Game Progress
遊戲進度		 Cat collection, happiness score, room decoration, daily task completion Save/restore gameplay, leaderboard ranking
Purchase Records
購買記錄		 In-app purchase transaction history (item name, price, date) Purchase verification, receipt matching, purchase history display
Device Information
裝置資訊		 Device model, OS version, app version Compatibility, bug fixes, performance optimization
User Preferences
使用者偏好		 Language selection, sound settings Personalized experience

2. Information We Do NOT Collect | 我們不收集的資訊

  • We do not collect location data. 我們不收集位置資訊。
  • We do not collect contacts or phone numbers. 我們不收集聯絡人或電話號碼。
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties. 我們不會將您的個人資料出售給第三方。
  • We do not use your data for advertising targeting. 我們不使用您的資料進行廣告投放。

3. Third-Party Services | 第三方服務

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In — Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In — Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — for in-app purchases
  • Firebase / CloudKit — for cloud save functionality

4. Data Storage & Security | 資料儲存與安全

Game data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to cloud storage (Firebase/CloudKit) when you enable cloud backup. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

遊戲資料儲存在您的裝置本機，當您啟用雲端備份時會同步至雲端儲存。資料透過加密連線 (HTTPS/TLS) 傳輸。

5. Children's Privacy | 兒童隱私

NyanVeil is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

NyanVeil 適合所有年齡。我們不會故意收集13歲以下兒童的個人資訊。

6. Account Deletion | 帳號刪除

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings > Delete Account within the App. Upon deletion, all cloud-saved data will be permanently removed.

您可以隨時透過 App 內的「設定 > 刪除帳號」刪除帳號及所有相關資料。

7. Changes to This Policy | 政策變更

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
