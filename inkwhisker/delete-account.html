Inkwhisker / 墨鬚貓 — Delete Account / Data

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Most players have no account. By default, Inkwhisker stores nothing on our servers — all data is in your own browser. If that's you, skip to "Clear local data" below.
大多數玩家沒有帳號。預設情況下，Inkwhisker 不會在我們的伺服器存任何資料 — 全部都在你自己的瀏覽器中。若你屬於這種狀況，直接看下方「清除本機資料」即可。

1. Clear Local Data (no account needed) | 清除本機資料

Open the game page, open your browser's developer tools (F12 on Windows / ⌥⌘I on macOS), go to the Console tab, and paste: 

localStorage.removeItem('inkwhisker_high_scores');
localStorage.removeItem('inkwhisker_settings');
localStorage.removeItem('inkwhisker_lang');

Press Enter. All local data is gone immediately. No server contact needed.

打開遊戲頁面 → 開啟瀏覽器開發者工具（Windows 按 F12，macOS 按 ⌥⌘I）→ 切到 Console 分頁 → 貼上上述指令 → 按 Enter。所有本機資料立即刪除，無需與伺服器聯繫。

2. Delete Cloud Leaderboard Entry | 刪除雲端榜單記錄

If you ever opted in to publish a score to the cloud leaderboard, you created a row containing: gamer tag, score, wave reached, ISO date, and browser locale (no IP, no other tracking).

To delete your cloud entry, email us:

  • To: fattyjay1224@gmail.com
  • Subject: Inkwhisker account deletion
  • Body: Your gamer tag (and ideally the email you used to sign up). No other information needed.

We will:

  1. Verify the request by replying to the email you provided.
  2. Delete the row from the high_scores table and the associated auth user from our InsForge backend.
  3. Confirm by email within 14 calendar days (usually within 48 hours).

Deletion is permanent. We do not retain backups of deleted leaderboard rows.

若你曾選擇上傳分數至雲端榜單，會建立一筆記錄包含：玩家名稱、分數、波次、ISO 日期、瀏覽器語系（無 IP、無其他追蹤）。請寄信至 fattyjay1224@gmail.com，主旨 Inkwhisker account deletion，內文提供你的玩家名稱（與註冊用的 email 為佳）。我們會驗證後刪除你的記錄與帳號，並於 14 個日曆天內以 email 確認（通常 48 小時內）。刪除為永久性，不保留備份。

3. Want to Export Your Data First? | 想先匯出資料？

Your cloud entry contains so little — just gamer tag, score, wave, date, locale — that we typically include the row contents in our deletion-confirmation email. Mention "please include row contents" in your request if you'd like.

你的雲端記錄非常少 — 只有玩家名稱、分數、波次、日期、語系 — 我們通常在刪除確認信中附上記錄內容。若你需要，請在請求中註明「請附上記錄內容」。
