Inkwhisker / 墨鬚貓 — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Inkwhisker (also known as 墨鬚貓) is a free browser-only web game. It runs entirely in your browser tab — there is no app to install, no account required to play, no ads, and no in-app purchases.

Inkwhisker（中文名稱「墨鬚貓」）是一款免費網頁遊戲，完全在你的瀏覽器中執行 — 不需要安裝、不需註冊即可遊玩、無廣告、無內購。

TL;DR | 一句話

We do not collect personal data. The only persistent data is your top scores and settings, stored in your own browser. Sign-up to the cloud leaderboard is entirely optional.

我們不收集個人資料。唯一保留的資料是你的最高分與設定，只儲存在你自己的瀏覽器中。雲端榜單登入完全是選擇性的。

2. Information We Do NOT Collect | 我們不收集的資訊

  • No cookies. (No consent banner needed.) 不使用 cookies，因此不需同意條款橫幅。
  • No third-party advertising. 無第三方廣告。
  • No fingerprinting, no tracking pixels. 無瀏覽器指紋識別、無追蹤像素。
  • No social login. 無社群登入。
  • No location, contacts, microphone, or camera access. 不存取位置、聯絡人、麥克風或鏡頭。
  • No sale of any data to anyone, ever. 永遠不會出售任何資料給任何人。

3. Third-Party Services | 第三方服務

The Game uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • InsForge (cloud leaderboard backend) — only used if you opt in to publish a score — insforge.dev
  • Plausible Analytics (cookie-less analytics) — Plausible Privacy Policy
  • Sentry (anonymous JS error monitoring) — Sentry Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Pages (static hosting) — Cloudflare Privacy Policy

4. Children's Privacy | 兒童隱私

The Game is self-rated PEGI 7 / ESRB E10+ for cartoon fantasy combat (no blood, no realistic violence). It has no chat, no user-generated content, no accounts, and no advertising. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and have any concerns, please contact us.

本遊戲自我分級為 PEGI 7 / ESRB E10+（卡通奇幻戰鬥、無流血、無寫實暴力）。無聊天功能、無玩家產生內容、無帳號制度、無廣告。我們不會故意收集兒童個人資訊。若您是家長並有疑慮，請聯絡我們。

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA) | 你的權利

Because we hold no personal data by default, there is usually nothing to delete, export, or correct. If you opted in to the cloud leaderboard, you may request deletion of your row — see Delete Account.

本遊戲預設不持有任何個人資料，通常沒有任何資料需要刪除、匯出或更正。若你曾選擇上傳雲端榜單，可申請刪除你的記錄 — 請見帳號刪除。

To clear all local data anytime, open your browser's developer console on the game page and run: 

localStorage.removeItem('inkwhisker_high_scores');
localStorage.removeItem('inkwhisker_settings');
localStorage.removeItem('inkwhisker_lang');
