Spirit Beast Ascending (靈妖也修仙) — Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account | 如何刪除帳號

Method 1: In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open the App
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

方法一：在 App 內（建議）：開啟 App → 設定（齒輪圖示）→ 往下滑 → 點擊「刪除帳號」→ 確認刪除

Method 2: By Email

Send an email to fattyjay1224@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your in-game display name or UID. We will process your request within 7 business days.

方法二：寄送電子郵件至 fattyjay1224@gmail.com，主旨為「帳號刪除申請」，並附上遊戲內顯示名稱或 UID。我們將在 7 個工作天內處理。

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All game progress, virtual items, and purchase history associated with your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

警告：帳號刪除為永久性操作，無法復原。您帳號關聯的所有遊戲進度、虛擬物品及購買記錄將在 30 天內永久刪除。

What Gets Deleted | 刪除的資料

  • Game progress (realm, inventory, pets, etc.) | 遊戲進度（境界、背包、靈寵等）
  • Account information (display name, linked providers) | 帳號資訊（顯示名稱、綁定的登入方式）
  • Purchase history records | 購買記錄
  • Friend connections | 好友關係
  • Sect membership | 宗門成員資格
  • Leaderboard entries | 排行榜紀錄

What is NOT Affected | 不受影響的項目

  • Your Google/Apple account itself | 您的 Google/Apple 帳號本身
  • App Store/Google Play purchase receipts (managed by Apple/Google) | 商店購買收據（由 Apple/Google 管理）
