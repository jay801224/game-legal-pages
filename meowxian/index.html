Spirit Beast Ascending (靈妖也修仙) — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Spirit Beast Ascending ("the App") is an idle cultivation RPG where you play as a cat on a journey to immortality. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

靈妖也修仙（「本應用程式」）是一款放置型修仙 RPG 遊戲，玩家扮演一隻修煉成仙的貓咪。本隱私權政策說明我們如何收集、使用及保護您的資訊。

1. Information We Collect | 我們收集的資訊

Data Type Details Purpose
Account Information
帳號資訊		 Display name, email (from Google/Apple sign-in), anonymous UID Authentication, cloud save identification
Game Progress
遊戲進度		 Realm level, spirit roots, inventory, pet collection, quest completion, cultivation stats Save/restore gameplay, leaderboard ranking
Purchase Records
購買記錄		 In-app purchase transaction history (item name, price, date), subscription status Purchase verification, subscription management, receipt validation
Device Information
裝置資訊		 Device model, OS version, app version, language setting Compatibility, bug fixes, performance optimization
Advertising ID
廣告識別碼		 Google Advertising ID (GAID) / IDFA (with user consent) Rewarded video ad delivery, ad frequency capping

2. Information We Do NOT Collect | 我們不收集的資訊

  • We do not collect location data. 我們不收集位置資訊。
  • We do not collect contacts or phone numbers. 我們不收集聯絡人或電話號碼。
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties. 我們不會將您的個人資料出售給第三方。
  • We do not use your data for personalized advertising targeting beyond rewarded ads you choose to watch. 除了您自願觀看的獎勵廣告外，我們不使用您的資料進行個人化廣告投放。

3. Third-Party Services | 第三方服務

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In — Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In — Apple Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Google Ads Privacy Policy (rewarded video ads only)
  • Google Play Billing / Apple StoreKit — for in-app purchases and subscriptions

4. Data Storage & Security | 資料儲存與安全

Game data is stored locally on your device and synced to Firebase Firestore when you sign in with a Google or Apple account. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Anonymous players' data is stored locally only.

遊戲資料儲存在您的裝置本機，當您使用 Google 或 Apple 帳號登入時會同步至 Firebase Firestore。資料透過加密連線 (HTTPS/TLS) 傳輸。匿名玩家的資料僅儲存於本機。

5. Children's Privacy | 兒童隱私

Spirit Beast Ascending is rated Everyone 12+ / PEGI 12 due to mild probability-based game mechanics. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

靈妖也修仙的分級為 12+（含輕度機率型遊戲機制）。我們不會故意收集 13 歲以下兒童的個人資訊。若您是家長且認為您的孩子提供了個人資訊，請聯絡我們。

6. Account Deletion | 帳號刪除

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings > Delete Account within the App. Upon deletion, all cloud-saved data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

您可以隨時透過 App 內的「設定 > 刪除帳號」刪除帳號及所有相關資料。刪除後，所有雲端資料將在 30 天內永久移除。

You may also request account deletion by email. See our Account Deletion page for details.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
