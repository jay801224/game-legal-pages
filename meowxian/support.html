Frequently Asked Questions | 常見問題

Q: How do I recover my account? | 如何恢復帳號？

If you signed in with Google or Apple, simply sign in again on your new device. Anonymous accounts cannot be transferred between devices.

如果您使用 Google 或 Apple 登入，只需在新裝置上重新登入即可。匿名帳號無法在裝置間轉移。

Q: How do I restore my purchases? | 如何恢復購買？

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Google/Apple account used for the original purchase.

前往 設定 > 恢復購買。請確認使用與原始購買相同的 Google/Apple 帳號登入。

Q: How do I delete my account? | 如何刪除帳號？

See our Account Deletion page for detailed instructions.

請參閱帳號刪除頁面了解詳細步驟。

Q: The app is not loading / crashes. | App 無法載入 / 閃退

Please try: (1) Force close and reopen the app, (2) Check for app updates, (3) Restart your device, (4) Reinstall the app (make sure your account is linked first!).

請嘗試：(1) 強制關閉並重新開啟 App，(2) 檢查 App 更新，(3) 重新啟動裝置，(4) 重新安裝 App（請先確認帳號已綁定！）

Bug Reports | 回報問題

When reporting a bug, please include: your device model, OS version, app version, and a description of the issue with screenshots if possible.

回報問題時，請附上：裝置型號、系統版本、App 版本、問題描述及截圖（如有）。
