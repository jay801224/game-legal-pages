FAQ | 常見問題

Q: How does MeowLinguistics interpret cat sounds? | MeowLinguistics 怎麼詮釋貓叫聲？

MeowLinguistics applies three independent academic models to your input: Moelk (1944) — classical phonetic patterns; Schötz et al. Meowsic (2019) — modern prosody analysis; Marvin Clark (1895) — Victorian-era 17 expressions. Results are interpretive, not literal. Every cat is unique — treat outputs as starting points, not verdicts.

MeowLinguistics 套用三個獨立的學術模型詮釋您的輸入：Moelk 1944（古典音素模式）、Schötz 等 2019 Meowsic（現代韻律分析）、Marvin Clark 1895（維多利亞時代 17 種表達）。結果為詮釋性質，並非字面翻譯。每隻貓都獨特 — 請將輸出視為起點，不是定論。

Q: Does the App need internet? | 需要網路嗎？

No. All translation logic runs on your device. The App opens an external Safari sheet only when you tap an academic citation link.

不需要。所有翻譯邏輯都在您的裝置上執行。只有在您點擊學術引用連結時，App 才會開啟外部 Safari 視窗。

Q: Where is my translation history stored? | 我的翻譯歷史存在哪？

On your device only, in Apple's SwiftData store. Nothing is uploaded. To clear it, see Account & Data Deletion.

僅存於您的裝置，使用 Apple 的 SwiftData。不會上傳。如需清除，請參閱帳號與資料刪除。

Q: Why does iOS ask about App Tracking on first launch? | 為什麼首次啟動時 iOS 要問是否允許追蹤？

iOS 14.5+ requires that any app capable of tracking show this prompt. MeowLinguistics does not actually track you regardless of your answer — we display the prompt because Apple's framework requires it.

iOS 14.5 起要求任何具追蹤能力的 App 都必須顯示此提示。不論您怎麼選，MeowLinguistics 實際上都不會追蹤您，我們只是因為 Apple 框架要求才顯示提示。

Q: The App crashed. What should I do? | App 當機了怎麼辦？

Please try the following:

  1. Update MeowLinguistics to the latest version from the App Store.
  2. Restart your device.
  3. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.

請嘗試：1. 從 App Store 更新至最新版本。2. 重新啟動裝置。3. 若問題持續，請以電子郵件聯絡我們，並附上裝置型號與 iOS 版本。

Q: How do I report a bug or suggest a feature? | 如何回報錯誤或建議功能？

Email us with a description, steps to reproduce (for bugs), and your device info. We read every message.

請以電子郵件聯絡我們，並提供問題描述、重現步驟（如為錯誤）及裝置資訊。我們會閱讀每一封來信。
