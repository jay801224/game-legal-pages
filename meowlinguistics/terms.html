2. What the App Does | 應用程式功能

MeowLinguistics provides research-based interpretations of cat language based on academic models (Moelk 1944, Schötz et al. 2019 Meowsic, Marvin Clark 1895). These are interpretations, not literal translations. Every cat is different; results are best-effort and indicative, not authoritative.

MeowLinguistics 根據學術模型（Moelk 1944、Schötz 等 2019 Meowsic、Marvin Clark 1895）提供學術依據的貓語詮釋。這些是詮釋，不是字面翻譯。每隻貓都不同，結果為盡力推論的指引性內容，並非權威性結論。
