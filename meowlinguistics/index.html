MeowLinguistics (貓語研究所) — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

MeowLinguistics ("the App") is a research-based cat language interpretation app. This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, where it lives, and how we protect your information.

MeowLinguistics（「本應用程式」）是一款根據學術研究詮釋貓語的應用。本隱私政策說明我們收集哪些資料、資料存放於何處，以及我們如何保護您的資訊。

Summary | 摘要

MeowLinguistics collects almost no data about you. We do not track you across apps, we do not sell anything to ad networks, and we do not have a backend that stores your translations. The only data that leaves your device is anonymous crash reports — and even those are sent only if you do not opt out via iOS Settings.

本應用程式幾乎不收集您的資料。我們不跨應用追蹤您，不會把任何資料賣給廣告聯盟，也沒有後台伺服器儲存您的翻譯結果。唯一會離開您裝置的資料是匿名的當機報告，且若您在 iOS 設定中關閉，連這個都不會送出。

2. What We Do NOT Collect | 我們不收集

  • Your name, email, phone number, or any account info (no account exists)
  • Your location
  • Your contacts, photos, or microphone audio
  • Browsing history or other apps installed
  • Advertising identifiers (we show no ads)

我們不收集：您的姓名、電子郵件、電話號碼或任何帳號資訊（本應用沒有帳號系統）；您的所在位置；您的通訊錄、相簿或麥克風錄音；瀏覽紀錄或您裝置上其他已安裝的應用；廣告識別碼（本應用不顯示任何廣告）。

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT) | App 追蹤透明度

iOS asks you on first launch whether to allow tracking across apps. MeowLinguistics does not track you across apps regardless of your answer. We display the prompt only because the iOS framework requires it — and your answer is respected either way.

iOS 會在您首次啟動時詢問是否允許跨應用追蹤。不論您選擇允許或不允許，MeowLinguistics 都不會追蹤您。我們顯示這個提示只是因為 iOS 框架要求，您的選擇都會被尊重。

4. Third-Party Services | 第三方服務

  • Apple App Store / TestFlight — for distribution
  • Apple's Crash Reporting — for diagnosing bugs
  • No third-party SDKs (no Firebase, no Google Analytics, no AdMob, no Facebook SDK)

本應用使用：Apple App Store / TestFlight（散佈）、Apple 當機回報（修錯）。不使用任何第三方 SDK（無 Firebase、無 Google Analytics、無 AdMob、無 Facebook SDK）。

5. Data Linked to Academic Citations | 學術引用連出之資料

When you tap a citation link in About / Onboarding (e.g., JSTOR for Moelk 1944), iOS opens that website in a Safari sheet. The website may set its own cookies — that is outside MeowLinguistics's control. See those sites' privacy policies for details.

當您點選 About / Onboarding 中的學術引用連結（例如 JSTOR 上 Moelk 1944 的論文），iOS 會在 Safari 視窗中開啟該網站。該網站可能設定自己的 cookie，這超出 MeowLinguistics 的控制範圍。詳情請參閱該網站的隱私政策。

6. Children | 兒童

MeowLinguistics is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it.

本應用不針對 13 歲以下兒童。我們不會故意收集兒童的任何資料。如您認為發生此情況，請聯絡我們，我們將立即刪除。

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA) | 您的權利

Because we don't collect linkable personal data, there is nothing on our servers to access, rectify, port, or delete. Your translation history is on your device — you control it (see how to clear local data).

因為我們不收集可連結至您身分的個人資料，所以伺服器上沒有資料可供您存取、更正、攜帶或刪除。您的翻譯歷史在您自己的裝置上，您完全自主掌控（清除本機資料的步驟請參考此處）。

8. Changes to This Policy | 本政策的變更

If we materially change what we collect (e.g., when Phase 2 adds Premium subscriptions), we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date and surface a notice in-app.

如我們實質改變收集的資料範圍（例如 Phase 2 加入 Premium 訂閱時），我們會更新本頁的「最後更新」日期，並於應用程式內顯示通知。
