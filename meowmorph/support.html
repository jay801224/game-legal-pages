FAQ | 常見問題

Q: How many free generations do I get? | 免費可以生成幾次?

You get 5 free generations every day. You can earn +1 more by watching a rewarded ad (up to 3 per day), or buy credit packs (1 credit = 1 image, never expires). Failed generations are never charged.
每天有 5 次免費生成,看獎勵廣告可 +1(每天最多 3 次),或購買點數包(1 點 = 1 張,永不過期)。生成失敗不扣次數。

Q: I bought credits but they didn't show up. | 買了點數沒入帳怎麼辦?

Credits are granted by our server after Apple confirms your purchase, usually within a few seconds. If they haven't appeared after a minute, force-close and reopen the app — the balance refreshes on launch. Still missing? Email us with your account email and we'll restore them.
點數是在 Apple 確認購買後由我們的伺服器發放,通常幾秒內到帳。若一分鐘後仍未出現,請強制關閉並重開 App(餘額會在啟動時更新)。仍未入帳請來信告知你的帳號 Email,我們會幫你補上。

Q: How do I restore my credits on a new device? | 換裝置後點數怎麼恢復?

Credit packs are consumable products, so they are NOT restored through the App Store's "Restore Purchases". Instead, your balance is stored on our server and tied to your account — simply sign in with the same Apple / Google / Email account on the new device and your credits appear automatically.
點數包屬於消耗型商品,不透過 App Store 的「恢復購買」恢復。你的餘額存在我們伺服器、綁定你的帳號——在新裝置用同一個 Apple／Google／Email 帳號登入,點數就會自動出現。

Q: Where are my generated images? | 我生成的圖在哪?

Open the "My Cats" (我的貓圖) tab to see your gallery. Free-tier images are kept for 30 days; images made with purchased credits are kept without an expiry.
開「我的貓圖」分頁即可看到圖庫。免費生成的圖保存 30 天;用點數生成的圖不設期限。

Q: How do I delete my account? | 如何刪除帳號?

Go to Settings > Delete Account (設定 > 刪除帳號). This permanently removes your account, photos, generation history and credits. This cannot be undone.
前往「設定 > 刪除帳號」。此操作會永久移除你的帳號、照片、生成紀錄與點數,無法復原。

Q: The app is crashing. What should I do? | App 當機怎麼辦?

1. Update MeowMorph to the latest version from the App Store. 2. Restart your device. 3. If it persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.
1. 從 App Store 更新到最新版。2. 重新啟動裝置。3. 若問題持續,請來信並附上裝置型號與 iOS 版本。
